Green Club is one of the clubs under the Science and Technology Council, IIT (BHU) gymkhana with a vision to maintain the greenery and sustainability of the environment in and around the IIT (BHU) campus.

  1. 1. Organized Clean Campus Drives which have received good participation from the students.
  2. 2. Shared a message to “switch off the lights when not in use” on every door of each hostel of our institute.
  3. 3. Posters and stickers on water coolers and wash rooms to reduce water wastage.
  4. 4. Flexes in hostel mess to spread awareness about food wastage.
  5. 5. Ban the use of disposable/single use plastic inside campus.
